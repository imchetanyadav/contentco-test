In brief: Unmanned retail shelves facing tough year in China

JD Daojia, JD.com’s online-to-offline supermarket platform, has downsized its unmanned shelf project “Go” as it focuses on the company’s online grocery and delivery businesses. This comes amid slowing growth in the unmanned retail shelf industry – a few startups in the country have already quit or pivoted to a new business model.
